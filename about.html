About me

Hi! I'm Nathan. I'm currently a software engineer at Apple in Cupertino.

I play and make games pretty avidly in my free time. My favorites are playable right here in your browser. I enjoy looking at everything that makes a game "fun"— from intricate gameplay systems that create depth, to little aesthetic touches that people hardly notice.
